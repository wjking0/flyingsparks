    Qualifications

    All work is carried out in accordance with the Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET) BS 7671:2018 wiring regulations (currently 18th edition). All of our technicians are either registered with NICEIC (the National Inspection Council for Electrical Installation Contracting) or another similar authorised competent person Part P self-certification scheme provider.
